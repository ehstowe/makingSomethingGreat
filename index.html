Why is visualization effective?

Visualization is an effective means to convey information because it often provides more truth than data alone. In The Truthful Art, Alberto Cairo retells a story of students who were happier to receive a 96/137 than a 72/100. A visualization of these numbers clearly shows that the 96 is a worse grade than the 72. Visualization allows users to better see relationships between pieces of data.
